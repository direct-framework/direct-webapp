Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we handle personal information on the Digital Research Competencies Framework website (directframework.com).

Accounts

You can choose to register for an account on this website. When you create an account we may collect your name, email address and other information you provide during registration. This information is used to manage your account, give you access to project resources, and support your participation in the community.

You may update or delete your account at any time. If you request deletion, your personal information will be removed from our active systems, although some records may be retained if required by law.

Information we collect

We only collect personal information if you choose to provide it, for example by registering an account, signing up to our mailing list or contacting us. This may include your name, email address or other details you provide directly.

How we use your information

We use the information you share with us to manage accounts, provide access to project resources, send updates about the framework and respond to enquiries. We do not sell or trade your personal information and we only share it when required by law.

Data storage and protection

We keep personal information only for as long as it is needed to provide services you have requested. We take reasonable steps to protect stored information from loss, misuse, unauthorised access or disclosure.

Third party services

This website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those sites.
