Get involved

The Digital Research Competencies Framework (Direct Framework) is a community driven, open and collaborative project. We welcome contributions from everyone, whether you are a researcher, a research software engineer, a data professional or someone with an interest in shaping the future of digital research skills. There are many ways to take part. You can join the conversation, share your expertise, suggest improvements or contribute directly to the framework.

Below you'll find links to our main community spaces. We'd love for you to get involved!

GitHub

Explore the code, suggest changes, raise issues, or contribute new ideas through our GitHub repository.

GitHub

Slack

Join the discussion, ask questions, and connect with others in the community on Slack.

Slack

Google Groups

Stay updated, take part in discussions, and help shape the framework through our Google Group.
